It's Worm Day!
2018-01-05

I'm Caz, and together with my partner Max we've just adopted some worms. This is mostly my blog about agriculture, food, worms, the environment, and potentially AI (coming soon :P) and how they can come together in a city. I have a masters in Environmental Science and a bachelors in Computer Science so am fairly well placed to shmush the two together! I'm also vegan and have been for almost ten years. I passionately believe that the future of food is in cities, will be fairly high-tech (whilst using what nature has honed over millions of years), and will be animal-product-free.

As a vegan how do I feel about having worms? They're not being exploited -- they're in their box of soil happily being fed. However if any research were to come up indicating the opposite, well, I'd set them free immediately. I believe we can feed the world without exploiting it.
